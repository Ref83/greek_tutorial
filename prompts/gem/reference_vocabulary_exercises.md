# Greek Vocabulary Exercise Reference

This document describes the types of Greek vocabulary exercises that can be generated for practice and evaluation.

---

# Exercise Types

## Multiple Choice (Grammar Recognition)

**Goal:** Identify the correct vocabulary form among several options.

**Type** select

**Example**

Ο Γιώργος είναι στο ___ (house) τώρα.

a) σπίτι  
b) γράφω  
c) είσαι  
d) γράμμα  

**Student Response Format**

1: a

**Explanation**

- Provide explanation of the correct vocabulary.

---

## Fill in the Blank (Grammar Production)

**Goal:** Produce the correct vocabulary item in correct form.

**Type** fill

**Example**

Εγώ ___ (write) ένα γράμμα.

**Student Response**

γράφω

**Explanation**

- Explain the correct form of vocabulary.

---

## Translation Exercise

**Goal:** Apply vocabulary in translation.

**Type** translate

**Example**

"We are reading a book."

**Student Response**

Διαβάζουμε ένα βιβλίο.

**Explanation**

- Explain the correct translation.
