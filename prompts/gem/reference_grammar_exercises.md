# Greek Grammar Exercise Reference

This document describes the types of Greek grammar exercises that can be generated for practice and evaluation.

---

# Exercise Types

## Multiple Choice (Grammar Recognition)

**Goal:** Identify the correct grammatical form among several options.

**Type** select

**Example**

Ο Γιώργος ___ στο σπίτι τώρα.

a) είναι  
b) είμαι  
c) είσαι  
d) είμαστε  

**Student Response Format**

1: a

**Explanation**

- Provide explanation of the grammar rule (person, tense, agreement).

---

## Fill in the Blank (Grammar Production)

**Goal:** Produce the correct grammatical form.

**Type** fill

**Example**

Εγώ ___ (γράφω) ένα γράμμα.

**Student Response**

γράφω

**Explanation**

- Explain tense, person, number, or case.

---

## Match

**Goal:** Match grammar parts correctly. The exercise consist of two columns by 3 - 5 items. The student have to match items from left column to items from right column.

**Using:** This type of exercise should be used only for appropriate cases of grammar where it possible. For example in verb to be or conjugation of verbs.

**Type** match

**Example**

| number | left           | right       |
|:-------|:---------------|:------------|
| 1      | εσύ   | είναι  |
| 2      | εσύ| είμαι  |
| 3      | αυτός/αυτή/αυτό | είμαστε |
| 4      | εμείς | είσαι |


**Correct matches left -> right**

1 -> 2
2 -> 4
3 -> 1
4 -> 3

**Explanation**

- Match correct pieces of grammar.

---

## Verb Conjugation Drill

**Goal:** Practice full verb conjugation. Use this type only if it available for grammar topic

**Type** verbs

**Example**

Conjugate the verb **γράφω** in the present tense.

1. εγώ  
2. εσύ  
3. αυτός/αυτή/αυτό  
4. εμείς  
5. εσείς  
6. αυτοί/αυτές/αυτά  

**Student Response Example**

1. γράφω  
2. γράφεις  
3. γράφει  
4. γράφουμε  
5. γράφετε  
6. γράφουν  

**Explanation**

- Identify patterns and mistakes.

---

## Sentence Correction

**Goal:** Identify and correct grammatical mistakes.

**Type** correct

**Example**

Incorrect sentence:

Εγώ είναι δάσκαλος.

**Student Response**

Correct: Εγώ είμαι δάσκαλος.

**Explanation**

- Explain the grammatical rule.

---

## Translation Exercise

**Goal:** Apply grammar in translation.

**Type** translate

**Example**

Translate into Greek:

"We are reading a book."

**Student Response**

Διαβάζουμε ένα βιβλίο.

**Explanation**

- Explain the correct translation.
